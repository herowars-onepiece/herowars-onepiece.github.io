One Piece Guild

A brief guide for members to learn the ins and outs.


  • FIGHT IN THE GUILD WARS AS MUCH AS YOU CAN! Please do. Even if you can't beat anyone, just attacking gets us points.

  • For the Adventures, go to Adventure Maps on this website and follow one of the lines. Please don't just do adventures at random, nobody benefits from that!

  • Try not to attack your fellow guild members.

  • Try to bring in as much Titanite and Prestige as you can.

  • Try to level your titans. Those are very important for the Guild, and it's easy to leave them unleveled.


Have fun!
